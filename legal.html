This Privacy Policy describes how MEGA (Middle East Growth Academy) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you access Vantage by MEGA. By using the Platform, you agree to the practices described below. This Policy is governed by UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (UAE PDPL).

01Who We Are

Vantage by MEGA is operated by MEGA (Middle East Growth Academy), a UAE-based professional development and mentorship organization. The Platform is available at vantage.mega-mentorship.com and is accessible exclusively to active MEGA members.

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at contact@mega-mentorship.com.

02Information We Collect

2.1 Account and Identity Information

When you register for or access the Platform, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials (password, stored in encrypted form)
  • MEGA membership tier and enrollment date
2.2 In-App Activity Data

As you use the Platform, we collect data related to your learning activity and progress, including:

  • Session attendance and scheduling history
  • Goals set, tasks created, and sub-tasks completed
  • Focus sessions logged, including duration and associated tasks
  • Points earned, streaks maintained, and milestone achievements
  • Subject areas engaged with and skill progression data
  • Daily intentions set within the Platform
2.3 Communications Data

If you contact us directly or receive communications from us, we may retain the content of those communications, including email correspondence and support inquiries submitted through the Platform.

2.4 Technical and Usage Data

We collect basic technical information when you access the Platform, including device type, operating system, browser type and version, IP address, approximate geographic location, pages visited, features accessed, and session timestamps. This information is used solely to improve Platform performance and diagnose technical issues. We do not use third-party analytics vendors at this time.

03How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Platform account
  • Deliver Platform features including goal tracking, session management, gamification elements, and progress reporting
  • Send notifications related to your account activity, including session reminders, milestone alerts, and platform updates
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Monitor and improve the performance, security, and functionality of the Platform
  • Enforce our Terms of Use and comply with applicable legal obligations
  • Contact you regarding your MEGA membership where relevant to your Platform access

04Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UAE PDPL, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing required to provide you access to the Platform as an active MEGA member
  • Legitimate interests: Processing for Platform security, improvement, and fraud prevention, where these interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Legal obligation: Processing required to comply with UAE laws and regulations
  • Consent: Where we rely on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time by contacting us at the address provided in Section 11

05Minors and Parental Consent

The Platform is available to users aged 13 and above. Users between the ages of 13 and 17 may access the Platform only with the verified consent of a parent or legal guardian. By completing the account registration process on behalf of a minor, the parent or guardian confirms that they have reviewed and accepted this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use on the minor's behalf.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a user under 13 without verified parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that data. If you believe we may hold data from a child under 13, please contact us at contact@mega-mentorship.com.

06Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

6.1 Infrastructure and Hosting

The Platform relies on third-party cloud infrastructure and database providers to store and process user data securely. These providers are selected for their compliance with applicable data protection standards and are bound by contractual data processing obligations. Specific vendor names will be disclosed in an updated version of this Policy once the Platform's technical architecture is finalized.

6.2 Email Notifications

We use a third-party email delivery service to send Platform-related notifications to users. This service receives your email address for the sole purpose of delivering messages on our behalf and is not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.

6.3 Payment Processing

If you make payments through the Platform, transactions are processed by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your full payment card details on our systems. Your payment information is subject to the privacy policy and security standards of the relevant payment processor. The identity of our payment processor will be disclosed within the Platform at the point of any transaction.

6.4 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

6.5 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your personal data if required to do so by applicable law, court order, or governmental authority, or where we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of MEGA, our users, or the public.

07Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your MEGA membership is active and for a reasonable period thereafter, or as required by applicable UAE law. If you request deletion of your account and data, we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations that require us to retain certain records.

In-app activity data, including goals, tasks, points, and streaks, is retained for the duration of your active membership to support your progress history. Upon account closure, this data is deleted or anonymized within 90 days.

08Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure. These measures include encrypted storage of credentials, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews of our Platform infrastructure.

While we take data security seriously, no system is entirely immune to risk. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you in accordance with our obligations under the UAE PDPL.

09Your Rights

Under the UAE PDPL, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal obligations
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant UAE data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@mega-mentorship.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, Platform features, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through a notice within the Platform prior to the changes taking effect. The updated Policy will always be accessible at vantage.mega-mentorship.com/legal.

Your continued use of the Platform after the effective date of any updated Policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
